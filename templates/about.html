About EnVol - Encourage Volunteering

It has been widely observed that when people are given some sort reward for their work, they tend to work harder to achieve them. Our application uses this very concept to encourage volunteers to participate in more events thus will be able to score more points and get e-certificates and Trophies that can be widely recognised by others.
